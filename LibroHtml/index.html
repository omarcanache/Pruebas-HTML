About BAWSI

The bay Area Women's Sports Intiative (BAWSI) is a public benefit, nonprofit coroporation with a mission to create programs and partnetships through which women athletes bring health, hope and wholeness to our commmunity. Founded in 2005 by Olimpic and World Cup soccer stars Brandi Chastain and Julie Foudy and Marlene Bjornrud, professional soccer team, BAWSI provides a meaningful path for women athletes to become a more visible and valued part of the Bay Area sports culture.
